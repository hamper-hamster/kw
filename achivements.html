My personal Wall of Pride, showcasing proud achievements over the years!!!


1. JLFest 2023, Sugoroku Merit Prize.


Referring to one of the projects mentioned in the Projects page, this is the first time participation of this competition, and it was a group competition with 4 members. So mentioning again, the merit prize was awarded because the first and second prize was forfeited due to what they say, the language standards were not up to their expectations. I would call this an achievement that was blindly achieved without much guidance on the entire competition flow which caused the files to be laggy due to the large number of blocks in it.


2. Selangor Youth Wushu Championship 2023, Champion in 15-17 years old male 65kg


This was my very first serious Wushu Sanda competition back then, I was fortunate enough to actually land on a category with only 2 people, which means I woul deither get second place or first place. To be honest, this competition was what further gained my expectations to myself in this sport in the future, and that was also when everyone started to have higher expectations on me as well as my potential. I mean yes, it does feel really proud to be able to achieve something this extraordinary at that time, which was probably why I would sometimes feel really unused to it and probably the start of my prime.


3. LFC Cup Selangor Wushu Invitation Championship 2023, Second Runner Up 15-17 years old male 56kg


Almost one month from the last competition, this one made me feel overwhelmed because as a beginner, suddenly having to face a national level competition, that was just a little too fast. This one was quite a pressure that sleeping became the only option of not thinking about it, although the reward after that was real unforgettable. At least I tried my best and although I did not win, I still managed to live up to people's expectations.


4. Kejohanan Wushu Selangor Jemputan 2024, First Runner Up 15-17 years old male 52kg


This competition, I didn't want to participate in it to be honest, the feeling of being overwhelmed was amplifying day by day to the extent that I probably couldn't take it anymore, I kind of forced myself to participate anyway. This competition was quite unforgettable because I was quite locked in back then, I pretty much bruised my left tibia and it was real pain in the leg. In the end, my legs couldn't take it anymore which made me lost the finals, therefore getting second place.


5. JLFest 2024, Sugoroku First Prize


This time, 1 year later, I gotten the First Prize, except my teammates have completely changed, because of my advantage of being able to use Scratch, I was able to participate in this competition again, this time my teammates have better command in terms of language which pretty much was the solution to the problem the year before. This was not as unserious as the previous year but I would say it was a very productive time as we had to focus on the competition and school exams simultaneously, which was something not everyone can handle. So yeah, this one is also another unforgettable moment in secondary school life.


6. Kejohanan "Sanda King" Malaysia Terbuka Kali Ke-2 2025, Bronze 15-17 years old male 60kg


The first serious Wushu Sanda competition in 2025, also the first time of being on such a large stage, I have no idea why but I was really not in good mental shape at that time, I basically felt the lack of confidence for some reason which I failed to comprehend. But I would say this was quite a nice experience, after the competition ended, we just enjoyed the hotel breakfast buffet and had fun at the swimming pool as well. So there is the reward after the exhausting part.


7. Selangor Wushu Invitation Championship 2025, Champion 15-17 years old male 65kg

Everytime when I think about this competition now, it feels like the clock for SPM is ticking already. This was one of the last few Wushu Sanda competitions I would participate in, at least I experience a nice moment before leaving the sport. First place in state level competition, I felt like I had the grip of it with the confidence to face the competition, compared to the previous year, that was a time where efforts are celebrated, imagine the next day walking in school like a champion, that was a great moment to feel.
