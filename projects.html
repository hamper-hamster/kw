2. Sugoroku World Food


This project, also made using Scratch, dates back to June to August 2024 was the first attempt to participate in the Sugoroku Competition in JLFest, it was a group competition and wow I gotta say it was a great testament to out teamwork and time management. Feels crazy how we survived day and night grinding this project with no actual knowledge of the competition details, we just kinda put our best in this project except we messed up the language part of the game. Weeks before the competition, we gathered a number of classmates to try out the game and gve feedback, in simple terms, 'beta testers'. Although we ended with merit prize, which was somehow second place as the first and second prize was cancelled, I gotta say we made it as a team, or should I say, second best in Malaysia. Thinking about those days, I get this nostalgic feeling which cannot be expressed with words.
